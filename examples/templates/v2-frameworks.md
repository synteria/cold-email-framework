# V2 Frameworks

Reusable frameworks derived from the validated v2 source. These are templates, not separate proof claims.

Live generated copy must omit the two flagged literal words documented in `CLAUDE.md` and `anti-patterns.md`.

## 1. Two-email Sequence: Same-Case Question Then Reason I Ask

### Email 1

Subject: quick question

Hi {{firstName}},

{{peerPatternOrPainSpecificFirstLine}}.

Same case for {{shortCompany}}?

Best,
{{sendingAccountFirstName}}

PS: {{personalizedLine}}

### Email 2

Subject: {{firstName}}?

Hi {{firstName}},

The reason I ask is I've been helping {{similarCompanies}} {{achieveResult}}, and want to do the same for you {{riskReversal}}.

Can I send a 5-minute Loom explaining how?

Best,
{{sendingAccountFirstName}}

## 2. Lead Magnet Plus Case Study

{{firstName}}: I made a {{leadMagnet}} for {{shortCompany}} on the {{strategy}} I used to help {{client}} generate {{result}} in {{timeframe}}. Mind if I send it?

Use when the case study is recognizable and the lead magnet is genuinely useful.

## 3. One-Liner Front-End Offer

{{firstName}}: interested in {{frontEndOffer}} for {{shortCompany}}?

P.S. {{socialProof}}

Use when the offer is so strong the whole email can ride on it. Name the deliverable, not the payment status, unless cost is central to the offer.

## 4. Direct Offer Plus Risk Reversal

{{firstName}}: interested in {{dreamResult}} for {{shortCompany}} in the next {{timeframe}}? Asking because my {{mechanism}} has done {{result}} for {{similarClient}}, open to it?

Use only when the sender can defend the claim on a call.

## 5. Pain Point Plus Solution

{{firstName}}: is {{specificPainOrPriority}} still a focus at {{shortCompany}}? I help {{ICP}} fix {{problem}} so {{endResult}}. Worth a look?

P.S. {{socialProof}}

Use when the pain point is concrete and non-generic. Avoid vague pain like "struggling with leads."

## 6. Relevant Touchpoint Plus Observed Issue

{{firstName}}: {{relevantTouchpoint}}.

Noticed {{specificObservedIssue}}.

Want me to send {{quickFix}}?

Use when the data is real: missing flow, slow site, broken ads, or a competitor doing something worth naming.

## 7. Unique Market Insight

{{firstName}}: {{questionAroundRelevantTouchpoint}}.

{{insightSpecificToTheirWorld}}.

{{CTAaroundImplementingIt}}

Use for tough or enterprise markets where insight is the wedge.
