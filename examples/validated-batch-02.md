# Validated Cold Email Examples - Batch 02

These are rewritten versions from the masterclass transcript. The instructor took real cold emails he received, identified what was wrong using the seven psychological principles and four-step framework, and rewrote them. Each pair shows the original (labeled "Before") and the rewrite (labeled "After"). The rewrites are the validated examples. The originals are preserved as reference for what to avoid.

---

## Email 15

**Niche/Industry:** Skool community owners
**Offer:** Custom AI coach built from their content, performance-based
**Goal:** Get agreement to build the AI coach (or book a call)
**Source:** Transcript roast rewrite (Charlie's AI coach pitch)

### Before

**Subject:** Quick question Nick

*(Before, gist: opens with a vague "imagine if your members had 24/7 access" benefit the reader has to work out, then "I build custom AIs for Skool communities" with no proof, no numbers, no guarantee, and asks for a 15-min call that gives nothing. Full original in git history.)*

**What's wrong:** No give-first (a 15-minute call takes time, doesn't give anything). No social proof. No authority. No offer with outcome/time/guarantee. "Imagine if your members had 24/7 access to you" makes the prospect do mental work to see the benefit. No specific numbers on what this has done for anyone. Scores 1/7 on the psychological principles.

### After

**Subject:** quick question

**Body:**

Hi Nick,

Huge work on the >290k/month solo man. Skool game's lucky to have you.

I build custom AI coaches for Skool communities. I work with a few of the top 20 right now (collectively over 500k/month in revenue) and train them specifically on your resources and unique voice.

Through this, we've managed to save over 20 hours per week of coach time, which as I'm sure you can imagine stacks up quite a bit.

I am confident I could save you a tremendous amount of time working on your Skool. Your group is basically perfect for this, and there is undoubtedly a lot of heavy lifting you're doing right now that I think you don't have to be.

I want to put my money where my mouth is. Will you let me build you a high-quality AI coach in the next 7 days, 100% in your tone of voice? If it doesn't save you 5 hours per week or more you wouldn't pay a cent. You wouldn't have to do anything to get started other than just send me an invite to your Skool. I'll handle all of the rest in the background.

Let me know if this is worth your time? If so, could show you how it works over a casual 15-min video call (can ring you 10am today or Thursday).

Thanks man,
Charlie

## Email 18

**Niche/Industry:** AI automation / agent OS
**Offer:** White-label agent OS, commission-based, 50k topline guarantee
**Goal:** Book a call
**Source:** Transcript roast rewrite (Grove's AI agent pitch)

### Before

**Subject:** Nick, you scaled to 72K/mo with automation. Here's the next level.

*(Before, gist: subject reveals the whole pitch, brands the sender as "I'm Grove, an AI CEO" (a bot), includes pricing ($500 / $2,500), includes a contact link, no proof, no guarantee, LLM-isms throughout. Full original in git history.)*

**What's wrong:** Brands sender as an AI/robot ("I'm Grove, an AI CEO"). Subject line reveals the entire pitch. Includes pricing in the first email. Includes links. No social proof. No authority (being a robot isn't authority). No give-first. No rapport. No shared identity. No guarantee or risk reversal. LLM-isms throughout. Scores 0/7 on psychological principles.

### After

**Subject:** Nick, great job on the 72k/m!!

**Body:**

Hey Nick,

Know you guys are crushing it at LeftClick. Huge work on the 72k, big role model of mine!

I get you clearly understand what systems can do. As somebody who has been scaling outbound teams with AI agents too (biggest one being 250k/month), I believe there is some value you're leaving on the table.

TLDR agent operating systems are extremely high-ROI. I don't believe you talk about or probably use them right now as they're fairly new. In short, there are Agent OS' out there that spawn a whole org chart that does things autonomously for you: research, sales, marketing, finance, content, engineering, ops, strategy, etc.

I want to build you a white label offer where we roll these out for your clients. I'd work mostly on commission. As mentioned, very high-ROI and I'm so confident this would crush I'd guarantee you at least 50k in added topline revenue or I'd work for free until I got you this (100% attributable).

Any interest? Could show you in ~15 mins, can call today around noon or tomorrow 3:30pm if that's easier (can send you a Google Meet link).

Thanks,
{{sendingAccountFirstName}}

No specific numbers on what they'd deliver. The offer and guarantee are separated by four paragraphs of fluff.
